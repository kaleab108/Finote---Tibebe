About Us

A little More About US:

"Finote Tibeb: Celebrating Ethiopian Cultural Traditions Through Poetry" - Finote Tibeb is a vibrant literary art center where Ethiopians come together to share and perform poetry that reflects their rich cultural heritage. From reciting ancient traditional poems to expressing contemporary thoughts and ideas, Finote Tibeb provides a platform for poets to connect with their roots and share their artistic expressions. Join us for an immersive experience of Ethiopian poetry, where you can witness captivating performances, engage in meaningful conversations about cultural traditions, and celebrate the power of words. Whether you are a poetry enthusiast or simply curious about Ethiopian culture, Finote Tibeb welcomes you to be part of this dynamic community that celebrates the beauty and diversity of Ethiopia's poetic traditions.
