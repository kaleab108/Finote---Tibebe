Our Events

Current Events

Ethiopian Poets' Symposium: A Celebration of Words and Culture" - Join us for an evening of captivating poetry, music, and cultural exchange at the Ethiopian Poets' Symposium. Explore the rich literary traditions of Ethiopia and engage in thought-provoking discussions on themes of identity, heritage, and contemporary issues. Open mic sessions will also be available for aspiring poets to share their work. Don't miss this unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the beauty of Ethiopian poetry!

"Rhythm and Rhymes: An Ethiopian Poetry Slam" - Calling all poets and spoken word artists! Get ready to showcase your lyrical prowess at the Rhythm and Rhymes Poetry Slam. Join us for a thrilling night of electrifying performances, where poets from across Ethiopia will compete in a friendly battle of words. From traditional rhymes to modern verses, witness the power of spoken word as poets captivate the audience with their talent and creativity.

Future Events

"Wax and Gold: Exploring Ethiopian Poetry Traditions" - Discover the ancient art of Ethiopian poetry at "Wax and Gold," a captivating event that delves into the country's poetic heritage. From the famous 'qene' poetry to the modern 'mead' poetry, immerse yourself in the rich oral traditions of Ethiopia. Listen to renowned poets recite their work, learn about the cultural significance of poetry in Ethiopia, and engage in thought-provoking discussions on the evolution of Ethiopian poetry over the years.

"Poetic Expressions: An Ethiopian Poetry Writing Workshop" - Unleash your creative spirit and join us for an interactive poetry writing workshop. Learn the nuances of Ethiopian poetry, experiment with different styles and techniques, and receive valuable feedback from experienced poets. Whether you're a budding poet or an experienced writer, this workshop will provide you with the tools and inspiration to express yourself through the power of words.
